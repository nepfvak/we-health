Your First Visit 診

Your first visit: ninety minutes, fully yours.

Most clinics schedule sixty minutes. We set aside ninety — enough time for a thorough consultation, a full physical assessment, and treatment, all in the same visit. Our practitioners take time to listen, explain, and address the root causes behind your symptoms, not just the symptoms themselves.

0′
30 minutes

Deep Listening

We begin not with forms but with conversation. Your primary concerns, your history, the texture of your symptoms — each detail is heard. We do not hurry toward diagnosis.

30′
Full evaluation

Complete Physical Assessment

A thorough review of medical history paired with hands-on physical evaluation. Pulse reading, palpation, a full-body survey of where the system holds tension and where it is depleted.

50′
Photographic analysis

Advanced Tongue Diagnosis

We photograph the tongue and walk you through what we observe — colour, coating, shape, moisture. Most practitioners assess and move on. We explain, so you leave understanding your own body.
