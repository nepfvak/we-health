I. Philosophy 道
氣
qì · vital breath

Acupuncture is not a treatment performed on you. It is a slow conversation with the body — listening for where breath has stopped, where warmth has thinned, where the current of qì has lost its road.

What we practise is older than memory: a medicine of pulse and palpation, of herbs gathered by season, of fine needles placed at the points classical texts have mapped for three millennia. It is patient work. It is meant to be. We do not chase symptoms — we tend to the soil they grew from.

II. Treatments 術

A small repertoire, practised deeply.

Four traditional modalities, offered alone or in combination. Each session begins with a pulse reading and conversation.

針

Acupuncture

zhēn jiǔ — 針灸

Sterile, single-use, hair-thin needles placed along the body's meridians to release stagnation, calm the nervous system, and restore the natural flow of qì.

灸

Moxibustion

ài jiǔ — 艾灸

The slow burning of mugwort over acupoints to warm the channels and tonify deficient cold. Often paired with needling for chronic fatigue and digestion.

Cupping & Gua Sha

bá guàn — 拔罐

Glass cups draw stagnant blood to the surface; jade tools clear the channels. Long used by physicians, dancers, and field workers alike for muscular ease.

III. The Practitioner 人
人 [ portrait of Dr. Selina ]
silver-gelatin tone · 4:5
選
Dr. Selina Wén-Èr
DACM PhD L.Ac. VA Authorized
Memphis, Tennessee · W&E Health Institute

Dr. Selina trained for nine years between Chengdu and the American South — first under her grandfather, a fifth-generation country doctor, and later in clinical residency at the Chengdu University of TCM. Her doctoral work spans Traditional Chinese Medicine and Western clinical research; her team holds both DACM and MD degrees.

She opened W&E in 2017 in a quiet brick storefront on Cooper Street because Memphis, she felt, needed a clinic that moved at the pace of a tea ceremony — patient, attentive, and entirely unhurried.

Lineage

Dr. Selina is the third-generation lineage successor of a specialised abdominal diagnostic tradition — and the first practitioner to carry it overseas. It is offered exclusively at W&E.
