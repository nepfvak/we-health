Dr. Yingjuan Liang (Dr. Selina)
DACM · L.Ac. · Tennessee · Oxford · Southhaven · Olive Branch, MS
  • Licensed Acupuncturist and Herbalist in Tennessee; doctoral degree in Acupuncture and Chinese Medicine; board-certified Diplomate of Oriental Medicine (NCCAOM)
  • Over the years, she obtained training from several prominent Chinese Medicine doctors in China and the United States
  • Deputy secretary general and executive director of the World Federation of Chinese Medicine Societies – Scalp Needle Committee
  • Member of China Association of Acupuncture-Moxibustion; holds certificate of "All-around of TCM techniques" issued by Chinese Bureau of TCM Administration
  • Direct disciple of Prof. Ying Wang in Modern Abdominal Diagnosis & Acupuncture — the only overseas practitioner to receive formal lineage recognition
  • Selected as one of twelve practitioners nationwide for the inaugural Overseas Chinese Medicine Mentorship, studying under Prof. Ming Jin in reproductive health & infertility
  • Clinically certified in Soliman Auricular Allergy Treatment (SAAT) by Dr. Nader Soliman directly
  • Member of the American Traditional Chinese Medicine Society (ATCMS) and the American Traditional Chinese Medicine Association (ATCMA)
  • Certified SAAT allergy treatment provider
  • Secretary-General, Mid-South Chinese Chamber of Commerce · Board Member, Greater Memphis United Federation · Co-Chair, inaugural 2025 Memphis Lunar New Year Fair (Miao Hui)
  • Yingjuan's parents are both doctors — she was deeply influenced not only by their skills for healing patients, but also their genuine personalities
  • Her practice prioritises treating patient's body, mind, and spirit with a holistic approach, especially at the spiritual level
