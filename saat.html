Soliman Auricular Allergy Treatment

SAAT is a specialized patented acupuncture technique, a gentle, non-invasive therapy designed to help reduce or eliminate allergic reactions. It is used to desensitize the immune system to various allergies, including Alpha-gal, food, environmental, and chemical sensitivities.

It involves inserting a tiny, specialized needle into the ear, often providing long-term remission. Most patients experience significant relief after a single session per allergen, with minimal discomfort and no need for ongoing drug therapy.
