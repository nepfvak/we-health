01 — Acupuncture

Acupuncture

針灸

Acupuncture is a key component of Traditional Chinese Medicine. Sterile, thin needles are inserted into specific points to enhance the vital energy flow and restore the balance of the body functions. It is widely known to be good for pain management. However, in the history of over 3,000 years in China and across the globe, it has been used for hundreds of conditions and diseases. It has gained recognition and popularity in recent years in the US and has been widely recognised as an alternative medicine.

02 — SAAT Allergy Treatments

SAAT Allergy Treatments

耳針脫敏

Soliman Auricular Allergy Treatment (SAAT) is a specialised patented acupuncture technique, a gentle, non-invasive therapy designed to help reduce allergic reactions. It is used to desensitise the immune system to various allergens, including Alpha-gal, food, environmental, and chemical sensitivities. It involves inserting a tiny, specialised needle into the ear, often providing long-term remission.

03 — Five Element Acupuncture

Five Element Acupuncture

五行針灸

Five Element Acupuncture is our special type of acupuncture rooted in ancient Chinese wisdom — five element theory. It is an effective and unique system to treat allergies and other root causes of diseases. It treats the body, mind, and spirit together. One of the most distinguishing values of Five Element acupuncture is its priority on treating at the level of spirit.

Herbal Medicine

中草藥

Studies have shown that Chinese herbal medicine is successful in treating a wide range of conditions. The ingredients include leaves, fruits, flowers, and roots of plants with great health benefits. We use concentrated natural plant granules and herbal pills to treat patients across multiple conditions, following the concept of Traditional Chinese Medicine theory.

05 — Cupping

Cupping

拔罐

Cupping therapy is an ancient form of Chinese medicine in which the practitioner puts special cups on your skin to create suction. It can help with pain, inflammation, blood flow, relaxation, and well-being. The famous swimmer Michael Phelps used cupping to release muscle tension.

06 — Moxibustion

Moxibustion

艾灸

Moxibustion is a type of Traditional Chinese medicine technique. Burning moxa, a special plant material, is burned over the surface of meridians and acupoints to improve the flow of Qi and circulation, and expel negative influences.

07 — Gua Sha

Gua Sha

刮痧

Gua sha is the practice of using a tool to apply pressure and scrape the skin to relieve pain and tension. It can improve circulation and is used to treat various musculoskeletal and immune conditions.
