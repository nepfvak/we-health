術Classical Modalities
01 — Acupuncture

Acupuncture

針灸

Sterile, thin needles inserted into specific acupoints to enhance vital energy flow and restore balance. Used for pain management and hundreds of conditions over 3,000 years of history — now widely recognised across the US.

Explore Acupuncture →
02 — SAAT Allergy Treatment

SAAT Allergy Treatment

耳針脫敏

Soliman Auricular Allergy Treatment (SAAT) is a patented auricular acupuncture technique that desensitises the immune system to allergens — including Alpha-gal, food, environmental, and chemical sensitivities — often providing long-term remission from a single tiny ear needle.

Learn more about SAAT →
03 — Five Element Acupuncture

Five Element Acupuncture

五行針灸

Rooted in ancient Chinese five-element theory, this unique system treats allergies and root-cause conditions by addressing body, mind, and spirit together. One of its most distinguishing qualities is its priority on treating at the level of spirit.

Explore Five Element Acupuncture →
04 — Herbal Medicine

Herbal Medicine

中草藥

Leaves, fruits, flowers, and roots with proven health benefits, prescribed as concentrated natural plant granules and herbal pills. Chinese herbal medicine has demonstrated success across a wide range of conditions, guided by Traditional Chinese Medicine theory.

Explore Herbal Medicine →
05 — Cupping

Cupping

拔罐

An ancient technique where special cups create suction on the skin to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, improve blood flow, and promote relaxation. We offer Sancai Fire Cupping — always included at no extra charge.

Explore Cupping →
06 — Moxibustion

Moxibustion

艾灸

Burning moxa — a specially prepared plant material — is applied over meridians and acupoints to improve Qi flow and circulation, and to expel negative influences. A deeply warming, restorative therapy.

Explore Moxibustion →
07 — Gua Sha

Gua Sha

刮痧

A tool is used to apply pressure and scrape the skin, relieving pain and tension while improving circulation. Used for musculoskeletal conditions, immune support, and deep fascial release.

Explore Gua Sha →

Training

培訓
